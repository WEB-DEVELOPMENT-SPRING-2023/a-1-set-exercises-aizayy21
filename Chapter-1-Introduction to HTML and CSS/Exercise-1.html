Aizah's Delicious Tortilla Pizza Recipe.

Tortilla Pizza is a genius quick meal! It forms the crust of this crispy pie, Ideal for an easy dinner or appetizer.

Main ingredients:

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • Olive oil or cooking spray
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • Â½ cup shredded cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Chopped basil

Method :

  1. Preheat the oven to 375F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Brush the tortillas with olive or spray with cooking spray.
  3. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce over each tortilla.
  4. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over each tortilla and top with desired toppings.
  5. Place the tortilla onto the lined baking sheet and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven, slice, and serve.
  7. If packing for lunches, allow the pizza slices to cool down to room temperature before packing into lunch containers.
