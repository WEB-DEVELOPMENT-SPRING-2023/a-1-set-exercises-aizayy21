PROGRAM OVERVIEW

This course celebrates digital creativity. We teach you broad computing and design skills,
from smartphone app building to VR game development and beyond. You will learn your craft
from the ground up, through a mix of workshops, tech demos, creative problem solving challenges,
and collaborative briefs. We believe in this field you will learn best by making, so you can look forward
To creating digital work from day one by various programe languages.

MODULE STUDY PLAN

You will be taught through creative workshops, technical labs, seminars and project drop-ins throughout year.
Digital Visual Design ,Digital Storytelling and Code Lab 1 in semister 1
Experience Design,Web Development and Ideation & Creative Problem Solving in semister 2
Code Lab 2,Games Development and Smartphone Apps strategies in semister 3
Emerging Technologies,Creative Industry Challenge and Responsive Web in semister 4
Machine Learning,Tomorrow's Web and Cyber Security in semister 5
Creative Incubator,Web Apps and Physical Computing in last semister.
