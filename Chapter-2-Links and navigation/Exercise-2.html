Wednesday


Plot

Wednesday Adam's family participates in their Nevermore Academy and pursues the vast adventure in the school where dark mysteries are hidden. Wednesday itself experiences high school, a first date, and definitely family trouble. Despite the fact that she learns she will ruin the academy, the academy is actually saved. In the path of her trip, she also discovered how to love her family and loved ones and how to fight the evil. She's even gifted with magical strength to aid her in her quest to battle the demon Hyde which eventually turns out her first love.
